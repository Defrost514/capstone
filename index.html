In an increasingly demanding world it is easy to lose focus and become distracted by an environment full of conflict and desires.

The most important step to warming up is a fun delicious and nutritious meal that is accessible to everyone.


WILD MUSHROOM MISO SOUP

INGREDIENTS

  • 100 grams matsutake mushrooms
  • 100 grams shiitake mushrooms
  • 100ml miso paste
  • 500 ml water
  • 25ml butter
  • 1 sliced green onion
  • 400 grams traditional tofu cubed

PROCEDURE

  1. fry mushrooms in butter.
  2. bring water to a boil, remove from heat and add miso paste and stir til combined (do not boil miso paste).
  3. place tofu fried mushrooms to bowls
  4. pour miso broth over mushrooms and tofuinto bowls
  5. garnish with chopped green onions
  6. enjoy!


FUSILLI RAGU WITH SPINACH

INGREDIENTS

  • 1000 grams ground beef and/or pork
  • 20 ml butter
  • 2 medium diced onions
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 large diced carrots
  • 3 spears diced celery
  • 2 large cans of diced tomatoes
  • 2 small cans of tomato paste
  • 20 ml italian seasoning
  • 1 bunch chopped spinach
  • cooked fusilli pasta
  • grated parmesan cheese
  • black pepper

PROCEDURE

  1. in a large oven safe pot or dutch oven hgeat butter and brown ground meat.
  2. reduce heat and add diced onions and minced garlic; cook until tender (add water if mixture becomes to dry, if the onions/garlic are not fully cooked when tomatoes are added the acid from the tomatoes will keep onions hard and crisp)
  3. add diced carrots and celery and cook to tender
  4. add cans of diced tomatoes paste and italian seasoning, combine ingredients and season to taste
  5. turn up heat and bring contents to a simmer stirring often
  6. place in oven for 3 hours stir hourly and check doneness (can be cooked on stovetop but requires greater attention and stirring as tomato based sauces/curries and prone to scorching)
  7. when done combine with cooked fusilli and fresh chopped spinach garnish with grated parmesan and black pepper
  8. chef's note: sauce can be cooked in advance; then heated in a skillet adding cooked pasta and spinach for individual portions

ROGAN JOSH WITH PEAS AND POTATOES

INGREDIENTS

  • 1000 grams seasoned lamb cubed
  • 30 ml ghee
  • 2 medium onions diced
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 50 ml curry powder
  • 2 large cans of diced tomatoes
  • 2 small cans of tomato paste
  • 500 ml chicken stock
  • plain yogurt
  • 500 ml cooked peas
  • 10 cubed potatoes boiled

PROCEDURE

  1. preheat oven to 150 celsius (300 Fahenheit)
  2. heat ghee in large oven safe stock pot or dutch oven, add lamb and brown.
  3. remove lamb and set aside, reduce heat and add diced onions minced garlic and curry spice and cook until soft (add water if mixture becomes too dry).
  4. return lamb cubes to the pot along with the cans of tomatoes, tomato paste, and chicken stock
  5. turn up heat and stirring often bring contents to a simmer
  6. place in oven for 3 hours stir hourly and check doneness (can be cooked on stovetop but requires greater attention and stirring as tomato based sauces/curries and prone to scorching)
  7. when finished cooking combine with potatoes peas and yogurt
  8. garnish with cilantro and serve with rice and naan
